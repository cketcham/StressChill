StressChill is a participatory sensing campaign geared towards identifying, describing and mapping the things you find stressing or relaxing in the places you spend your time everyday.

With this Android application you can view a map of the things participants find affect them as well as upload your own observations.

The goal of this application is to map the things in your environment that you think need attention or to share the places you find relaxing with everyone else.
